FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before launch.

When does More Success Academy launch?

We’re actively building and plan to launch our beta phase soon. Waitlist members will be the first to get access — often weeks before the public release.

Is this only for Nigerian learners?

No — More Success Academy is designed for learners across all of Africa. While we start with a Nigerian focus, we are pan-African in vision and will serve every country on the continent.

How much will it cost?

We’re building with affordability in mind — flexible monthly plans, pay-per-course options, and micro-certifications priced for African markets. Waitlist members will receive special founding-member discounts.

What kind of courses will be available?

Tech skills (data, AI, cybersecurity, software), business & entrepreneurship, English proficiency, career development, secondary school support, scholarship preparation, and corporate training tracks — all in one unified platform.

Are you looking for investors or partners?

Yes! We’re actively seeking strategic investors, EdTech partners, and content collaborators who share our vision of transforming African education. Reach out at info@moresuccessacademy.com.ng .
